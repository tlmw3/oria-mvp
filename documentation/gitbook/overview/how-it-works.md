# How it works

A new user goes from "first install" to "earning" in four short steps.

## 1. Sign in

Privy embedded wallets. Email, Google or Apple — no seed phrase, no extension needed. A non-custodial wallet is created on the spot.

## 2. Pick a name + photo

You confirm or edit the name Privy provides (e.g. from your Google account) — Oria needs at least 2 characters so friends can find you on the leaderboard. A photo is optional; if you skip it, you get a neutral silhouette placeholder.

## 3. Set a weekly goal

Choose a discipline (running, cycling, steps) and a weekly target in km. Optionally connect Strava so runs sync automatically; if you skip Strava, you can still log km by hand each week.

## 4. Deposit & invest

Fund your Oria wallet with USDC, then invest into one of three curated Morpho vaults:

* **Steakhouse Prime USDC** on Base (default)
* **Gauntlet USDC Prime** on Base
* **Gauntlet USDC Frontier** on Ethereum

Withdraw whenever you want. Your funds stay in ERC-4626 vault shares.

***

## What happens each week

* You hit your weekly km target → **streak +1**, you stay in the active pool.
* You miss the target → **streak resets to 0**, you drop back to the 3 % baseline.
* A weekly evaluator recomputes everyone's activity score, the global mean, and each user's share of the bonus pool.

The dashboard surfaces the result in real time: your streak count, your current week progress, your effective APY, and a feed of what your friends are pulling off.
