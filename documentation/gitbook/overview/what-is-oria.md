# What is Oria

Oria is a mobile-first PWA that bridges **DeFi savings** and **personal fitness goals**:

* You deposit USDC.
* You log your weekly running, cycling, or step goals (Strava sync or manual).
* Your **consistency** drives a redistribution bonus on top of a guaranteed 3 % baseline APY.

The yield itself comes from curated Morpho vaults on Base and Ethereum mainnet — Oria isn't generating yield from thin air, it's just modulating how that yield is distributed among its users.

## Why it exists

DeFi yield products typically pay everyone the same rate. That ignores user behavior — the most active, loyal users get the same return as someone who deposits once and forgets. Oria reframes savings as a habit:

* **Stay consistent** → bigger slice of the bonus pool.
* **Stop showing up** → drop back to the guaranteed baseline.

The fitness link makes consistency tangible and verifiable. Your Strava data (or manual log) is the proof.

## Who it's for

* Crypto-native savers who want a more engaging product than a flat-yield vault.
* Fitness enthusiasts who want their habit to translate into a financial reward.
* People who like the gamification of streaks (Duolingo, Strava clubs, Apple Fitness rings) and want that same dynamic on their money.

## What's not here

* No leverage, no swaps, no token of our own — just savings + activity.
* No custody risk we created — your USDC stays in Morpho's ERC-4626 vaults.
* No social pressure to share — the activity feed is private to your friends, you choose who you connect with.
